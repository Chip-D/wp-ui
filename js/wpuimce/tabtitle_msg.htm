How to choose tab titles.

Choosing tab titles can be done through three methods

  • Select a text, and click wptabs icon -> Tab Name. Selected text will now be wrapped with shortcode [wptabtitle].
  • In the toolbar, Click wptabs icon -> Tab name. On the upcoming prompt, please input your tab's title. Now click ok, that's it.
  • If this seems too much for you, you can just switch to HTML mode in the wordpress post editor, and add [wptabtitle] before and [/wptabtitle] after your text.
